Welcome to the QUAD SQAD

QUAD SQUAD is a social media website mockup with a locally hosted back end created by Alyssa Jones, Tefe Del Rosario-Bell, & Ashley Joseph. Users could create an account, log in, create posts & comments, delete their own posts & comments, & view their profile. Since QUAD SQUAD is not deployed anywhere, screen shots of a sample user visit are posted below.
